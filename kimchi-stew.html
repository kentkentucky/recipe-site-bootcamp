Kimchi Stew

Ingredients

Serving size: 3













Preparation Time

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
10 minutes 25 minutes 35 minutes

Instructions

  1. Place the kimchi and kimchi brine in a shallow pot.
  2. Add pork and onion.
  3. Slice 2 green onions diagonally and add them to the pot.
  4. Add salt, sugar, hot pepper flakes, and hot pepper paste.
  5. Drizzle sesame oil over top.
  6. Add the anchovy stock.
  7. Cover and cook for 10 minutes over medium high heat.
  8. Open and mix seasonings with a spoon. Lay the tofu over top.
  9. Cover and cook for another 10 to 15 minutes over medium heat.
  10. Chop 1 green onion and put it on top of the stew
  11. Remove from heat and serve right away with rice.
